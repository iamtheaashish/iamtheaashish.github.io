2. I am poor. And I want to elaborate on this.

---
I am poor so I can't afford gym membership, you can say whatever you want, you can advice me to home-workout, lift bricks, do pushups with SL Arora & RD Sharma in bagpack. Which is reasonable for what these places offer.

I am poor, that's why I can't afford high quality, protein rich diet. Right now, I am sustaining myself primarily with soyachunks, and eggs few times in a month. Good thing about soyachunks is that they are cheap, bad thing is that bio-availablity is poor. And they cause bloating. There have been instances when my gut health has become worse and I have nearly died by consuming just soaked soyachunks without boiling. Even after boiling and eating it with just simple salt, I face bloating and gas issues regularly. So here you can't give advice to me. My father earns 18k a month(you subtract fuel expenses and his leaves), yes living in Delhi. I am grateful for it, although I have to change my circumstances.
